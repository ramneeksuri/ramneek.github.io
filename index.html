01 · What drives me

The right answer comes from context, not just codification.

Getting the ASC citation right is table stakes. The real value sits at the intersection of technical accounting and business reality — and, increasingly, in knowing what to hand to an agent and what to hand to a partner.

“
I lean heavily on AI to handle the volume and the repetitive layers of the work, so my time goes toward what actually requires judgment, experience, and a seat at the table.

The work I find most rewarding sits at the intersection of technical accounting and business context. Understanding why a company structured a deal a certain way — what the business implications are, and how the accounting treatment should reflect that reality — is where the real value shows up.

I’ve built agents for contract review, pipelines for diligence templating, and LLM workflows that automate the repetitive layers of an engagement. That frees senior capacity for the judgment calls clients actually hire us for.
